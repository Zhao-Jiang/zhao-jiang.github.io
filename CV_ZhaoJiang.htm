Research experiences and interests

My previous research experiences were mainly field-scale agricultural remote sensing with UAV for plant stress phenotyping and crop growth monitoring. I’m also interested in multi-scale agricultural remote sensing with airborne, space-borne, and satellite imagery. Using field measurement, spectroscopy analysis, radiative transfer modeling, machine learning, and computer vision algorithms for vegetation stress detection, crop traits monitoring, yield prediction, agroecosystem variables monitoring, and precision agriculture applications.

Software used

Python, MATLAB, Keras with TensorFlow backend, Ubuntu, ArcGIS, ENVI, Agisoft PhotoScan, Pix4D mapper, Google Earth Engine (basic).

Hardware used

UAV platforms: DJI Matrice 600 series, Matrice 300, and Phantom 4 RTK;

Imaging sensors: Hyperspectral imaging sensor (Headwall nano, Cubert S185 snapshot), MicaSense RedEdge multispectral sensor, DSLR RGB cameras.

Handheld sensors: ASD FieldSpec, PSR+ Spectroradiometer, LAI-2200C, LP-80, Trimble GreenSeeker, GNSS RTK instrument.
